# Workspace Language Policy

All documents, code, code comments, commit messages, and architectural artifacts must be written in English.

## Rationale

This policy improves:
- clarity across teams and contributors,
- consistency across technical artifacts,
- long-term maintainability,
- onboarding efficiency,
- LLM operability and deterministic automation.

## Scope

This policy applies to all future contributions in this workspace, including automated contributions.

## Enforcement

Claude must follow this policy in all future turns unless explicitly overridden by the user.
